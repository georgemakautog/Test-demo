ABOUT US

We are the BTVTEd-CSS Community dedicated to creating accessible learning tools for Computer Systems Servicing students. Our platform provides interactive 3D models, simplified definitions, and hands-on guides to support learners of all levels.

Our goal is to make complex computer components easier to understand through visual aids, mobile-friendly content, and student-centered learning approaches.

Whether you are a beginner or advancing your ICT knowledge, this platform equips you with the necessary tools to grow in the field of Computer Systems Servicing.
