What is a Motherboard?

The motherboard is the main printed circuit board (PCB) inside your computer. It connects and allows communication between all essential components such as the CPU, RAM, storage drives, GPU, and input/output devices.

It serves as the central backbone of the system by ensuring everything works together harmoniously. Without a motherboard, none of the components can communicate or function properly, making it one of the most vital parts of every computer.
